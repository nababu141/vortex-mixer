How it Works

1. Generate: Create a unique, non-custodial vault address on the BASE network.

2. Deposit: Send ETH to your vault. The funds are mixed within our liquidity pool.

3. Anonymize: Your transaction history is detached from your identity.

4. Withdraw: Use your Private Key to send funds to a new, clean address.
